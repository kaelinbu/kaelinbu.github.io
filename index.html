PROJECTS

LocalBeat is a mobile-first app that lets you visually search for concerts on an interactive map, preview the band's top song, and buy tickets. If you're a music lover looking for something to do in your own city, or while traveling, LocalBeat will allow you to find with your favorite bands' shows and discover new music along the way. This single page app is client heavy, using mostly Javascript, a lot of AJAX and minimal Ruby. It incorporates 5 different APIs.

Moodstagram creates collages of images (pulled from the Instagram API) based on a rotating selection of "mood buttons". This single page Sinatra app utilizes AJAX, background jobs, and solid OO principles.

Fortune Tweller is a simple Twitter clone (with a fortune cookie twist) using Sinatra.
